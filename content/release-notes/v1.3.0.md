## Persistent history (Pro)

- Analysis history now persists across sessions and devices for Pro users
- Previous sessions appear in the sidebar under "Previous sessions" with mode, audience, date, and input preview
- Click any entry to restore its full workspace (input, output, mode, audience, instructions)
- Delete individual entries or clear all history from the sidebar
- Free tier users see session-only history with an upgrade prompt
- Server-side storage with newest-first ordering; entries are saved automatically after each analysis

## Review accuracy: title case

- Review mode now understands title case rules: short prepositions (in, on, at, to, for, of, with, by, from, up), articles (a, an, the), and coordinating conjunctions (and, but, or, nor) stay lowercase unless they are the first or last word
- Eliminates false positives like flagging "Template Visibility Issues in Backstage" as incorrect
